Audi R8

The 2023 Audi R8 packs a 5.2-liter V10 engine with two states of tune: 562 horsepower and 602 horsepower. A seven-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive are standard; Quattro all-wheel drive is available but only with the latter configuration.

Audi RS Q8

The RS Q8 has a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V-8 engine that produces 591 horsepower and 590 pound-feet of torque compared with the SQ8's 500 horses and 568 pound-feet.

Audi A5 Coupe

Highs A rewarding drive, punchy turbo four-cylinder, generous cargo space. Lows No manual transmission option, narrow front seating area, options add up. Verdict The two-door A5 is like a better-looking A4 sedan, but with less rear seat space.
